The Best Movies According to Ankit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This is a really great movie about life, love and friends.

The Adam Project

Comes under the sci-fi genre and really cool concept about time travelling is shown in this movie.

Spider-man : No Way Home

Out of all the marvel movies I found this one to be really emotional, and it also combines all three of the spider-mans which also adds a really great nostalgic factor to the movie as well.
